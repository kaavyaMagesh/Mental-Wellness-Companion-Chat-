Frequently
asked
questions

What is InnerWhispers? +

InnerWhispers is an AI-powered wellness platform that supports emotional well-being through conversations, journaling, mood tracking, and wellness insights.

Who is Wisp? +

Wisp is the AI Wellness Companion developed by InnerWhispers to support reflection, emotional awareness, and personal growth.

Is Wisp a therapist? +

No. Wisp supports self-reflection and wellness but does not provide medical or psychological treatment.

Is my data private? +

Yes. Protecting your privacy and confidentiality is a core priority, and your information is handled with care.

How does Wisp provide insights? +

Wisp analyzes your journal entries, emotional themes, and reflection patterns to generate personalized wellness insights.

Can I delete my data? +

Yes. You maintain control over your information and can delete it at any time.
