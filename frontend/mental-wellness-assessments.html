Disclaimer

These assessments are intended for educational, wellness, and self-reflection purposes only. They do not provide medical diagnoses and should not replace professional mental health care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Frequently
asked
questions

Are these assessments clinically validated?+

Yes. Tools such as PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS, AUDIT, WHO-5, and PSQI are widely used, evidence-based screening instruments. However, results are for self-reflection and informational purposes only, not a clinical diagnosis.

How long does each assessment take?+

Most assessments take between 1 and 5 minutes to complete, depending on the number of questions involved.

What happens after I complete an assessment?+

You'll receive an instant score with a clear explanation, along with personalized wellness insights and resources from your AI Wellness Companion.

Is my assessment data kept private?+

Yes. Your responses and results are securely stored and handled with care, in line with our privacy and data protection practices.

Can these assessments replace therapy or a diagnosis?+

No. These tools are intended for self-reflection and wellness monitoring only. They do not provide a medical diagnosis and should not replace professional mental health care or treatment.

What if my results indicate a concern?+

If your results suggest an area of concern, we recommend discussing them with a qualified mental health professional. If you are in crisis or at immediate risk, contact local emergency services right away.
