# Sleep Hygiene

Topic: Sleep Hygiene
Version: 1.0
Review Status: Cleaned
Audience: General Public
Safety Level: Wellness

## Overview
Sleep hygiene refers to daily habits and environmental factors that support consistent, restful sleep.

## Helpful Habits
- Keep a regular sleep schedule
- Create a comfortable sleep environment
- Limit stimulating activities close to bedtime
- Reduce caffeine late in the day
- Develop a calming bedtime routine
- Get natural light exposure during the day
