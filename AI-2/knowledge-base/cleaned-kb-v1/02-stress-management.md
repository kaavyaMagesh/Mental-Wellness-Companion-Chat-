## Overview
Stress is a normal part of life. Learning healthy coping strategies can help people respond to challenges more effectively and maintain overall well-being.

## Practical Strategies
- Maintain regular sleep habits
- Stay physically active
- Spend time with supportive people
- Take breaks during demanding periods
- Practice relaxation or breathing exercises
- Make time for enjoyable activities
- Organize and prioritize tasks

## Quick Reflection
1. What is causing stress right now?
2. Which parts can I influence?
3. What is one small step I can take today?
