## Overview
Mindfulness is the practice of paying attention to the present moment with openness and curiosity.

## Everyday Techniques
- Mindful breathing
- Body awareness exercises
- Paying attention during routine activities
- Brief pauses throughout the day
- Observing thoughts without immediately reacting to them
