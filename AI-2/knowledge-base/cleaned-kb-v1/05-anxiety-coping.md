Topic: Anxiety Coping
Version: 1.0
Review Status: Cleaned
Audience: General Public
Safety Level: Wellness

## Overview
People may experience worry, nervousness, or feelings of uncertainty during challenging situations. Practical coping skills can help individuals respond more effectively.

## Coping Skills
- Slow, steady breathing
- Grounding exercises
- Physical activity
- Talking with trusted people
- Limiting information overload
- Breaking large tasks into smaller steps
