## Knowledge Base Taxonomy
### CBT Basics
Foundational articles explaining cognitive behavioral therapy, its core principles, and practical CBT-based tools for thought and behavior change.

### Stress Management
Sources focused on healthy coping strategies, stress reduction habits, and approaches for handling acute and chronic stress.

### Sleep Hygiene
Articles covering sleep routines, sleep behaviors, and environmental habits that support healthy rest and recovery.

### Mindfulness
Content on present-moment awareness, meditation, mindfulness-based stress reduction, and mindfulness as a coping skill.

### Anxiety Coping
Educational resources on anxiety symptoms, self-help strategies, grounding, exposure, and coping techniques.

### Depression Awareness
Awareness-oriented materials on depression symptoms, related mood conditions, and when to seek professional support.

## Content Acquisition Standards
- Trusted Sources Only.
- Educational Content.
- Non-Diagnostic Content.
- Wellness-Focused Guidance.
- Evidence-Informed Information.
- Publicly Accessible Sources.
