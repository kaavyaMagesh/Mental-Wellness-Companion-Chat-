About the PSS-10

Developed by Cohen, Kamarck & Mermelstein (1983). This is a validated psychological tool for measuring perceived stress, not a clinical diagnosis. Scores reflect the past month and may vary over time. If you are concerned about your mental health, please consult a qualified professional.
